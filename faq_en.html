Frequently Asked Questions

Here are answers to common questions about our project and activities.

What is HilmarCorp?

HilmarCorp is a research & development laboratory dedicated to quantitative finance. We do not offer financial products to the public and our work is exploratory.

Are you regulated?

No. As a laboratory, we are not authorised to provide asset management or investment advice services. However, we respect regulatory frameworks related to data protection.

When will your fund launch?

We are in a phase of research and experimentation. No launch date has been set and we will communicate in due course.

How can I collaborate with you?

We are open to academic and industrial partnerships. You can contact us via the form on the home page or sign up for our newsletter to stay informed about opportunities.
